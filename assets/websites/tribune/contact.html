Frequently Asked Questions

How it works

What is Advertising Self Service?

The Los Angeles Times’ Advertising Self Service website is the simplest and fastest way to get your advertising campaign running in print and online. Tailored for small businesses and individuals, it allows you to place many types of ads: Print Display, Online, Classified, etc. By browsing our website {hyperlink to home page}, you can find the advertising campaign that best suits your needs. You construct your ad from start to finish and choose where it runs to maximize the exposure to the segments of our broad audience you're looking to target.

Why choose Self Service?

Our self service site offers small advertisers and individuals the quickest and most cost effective way to place an advertisement or announcement in one of our many products. You will find the lowest rates, a site that is available whenever you need it and the flexibility to customize a campaign that best suits your needs.

Do I have to build my ad from scratch?

We offer pre-built templates that allow you to input text and/or upload images. They range from very simple, to very detailed ads, that we’ve hand-picked to stand out or best match the environment when run in our paper or on one of our websites. Also, for nearly all advertising categories, we allow you to upload your own material. If you’ve done some great work in-house, simply upload it, we’ll make sure it fits the size you’ve selected.

How do I place an ad?

It’s very simple. Start here on our Ad Options Page {hyperlink to page} and find the product or section that best fits your needs as an advertiser or individual. If you’re not sure, we offer details on each product under “What you get” and for many products, you can click on “More Info” to learn more. When you’ve found the right product or section, click the purchase button and you’ll be launched into the workflow to build, target and schedule your ad. Once you’ve finalized everything, proceed to the checkout screen, pay, and your ad will run as you’ve specified.

Managing my ads

Do I need to create an account?

Yes, we ask that you create an account {hperlink to create an account screen] with us when purchasing an ad. This allows us to make sure that you have full access to all of your orders, you can update anything in your material that you might need to prior to deadline, you can save and access drafts and you can easily renew previously placed orders. Creating an account is simple and takes less than a minute. However, feel free to browse our products and build ads without registering in order to get a feel for how our Self Service website works. If you need more information regarding our Privacy Policy.

Am I able to upload photos and graphics to my ads?

Yes, all dsiplay ads and some classified ads will allow you to upload photos and graphics. Our classified partner sites such as cars.com and homefinder.com will also allow you to upload photos for the listings on their sites. Also, for many products we allow you to upload your own ad material without using one of our pre-built templates.

Do you offer the ability to target my ad to a specific region of Los Angeles?

Yes, several different products and sections provide flexibility to target specific geographic regions of the Los Angeles Area. These range from print sections {hyperlink to print zoned sections?}, to online sections {hyperlink to geotargeted online sections?} that serve various areas of Southern California.

How far in advance do I need to submit my advertisement in order for it to run?

This depends on the product you choose for your advertising solution. Some products allow for next day publishing, while others require more than a week advance submission. After you select a product to run, we will display a calendar that will show you the next available day for your advertisement to run in print and/or online.

How do I update my ad once it has been placed?

After you place your ad, you will receive an email confirming that you've submitted an ad. In this email, there is a link to review your ad that takes you directly to your order, where you can edit, renew or cancel it. Also, if you login upon returning to our website, you can browse all your ads that are currently running, have run and/or are saved as drafts.

Payment Options

What forms of payment do you accept?

All of our self service products are paid in advance with a credit card. We accept Visa, MasterCard, Discover & American Express.

Troubleshooting

What if I forget my username or password?

To retrieve your login information and/or password, simply click on “Forgot Password”. The site will walk you through the steps required to reset your password and give you instant access to your account upon completion.

I can't find my ad in print and/or online

In most cases, your online ad appears the same day your print ad is in the paper. If you cannot find your ad, please call us at 1.800.234.4444, ext. 73445or email us at myad@latimes.com and one of our representatives will assist you.
